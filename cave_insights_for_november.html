Test Drive Next Year's Cave Menu!

 

Introducing a More Intuitive Navigation

We're excited to give you early access to our revamped menu system that'll be the default starting January 1. This enhanced navigation keeps the second row static, making it easier for you to jump between top-level feature sets without having to return to the Main Menu. Once you select a feature set, you'll see a submenu below it that's also static, streamlining your navigation even more.

 

Your Feedback Matters

We're in the polishing stage and would love to get your feedback. Feel free to toggle between the new "Menu 2024" and the current "Standard Menu" by clicking the © symbol in the top right of the Cave menu. We're all ears for any suggestions or issues you encounter.

 

Future Focus

While we'll continue to refine the 2024 Menu based on your feedback, rest assured that the current "Standard Menu" will remain an option for those who prefer it.

Introducing Tap-to-Add on Satellite

Simplify Your Ordering Experience

We've just made adding items to your orders on mobile simpler than ever! With our new "Phone v2" view, you can now use ➕ and ➖ buttons to quickly add bottles and cases—no need to bring up the keyboard!

 

How to Get Started

Simply select an order on Satellite and switch to the new "Phone v2" view from the View dropdown. You'll find it not only includes these new 'quantity stepper' buttons for units and cases but also offers other mobile optimizations.

 

Try It Now

We encourage you to try this new feature and share any feedback.
